About me


Hi and welcome!

My name is Edgars and this is my portfolio. Here I post various projects that I have been working on while doing my Multimedia Design studies at the Copenhagen School of Design and Technology (KEA).

My biggest passions in life are music, writing and visual/production design.

I have a large interest and excitement when it comes to developing ideas and concepts for UX/UI design. I love the process of collaboration with a client, hearing out their vision and needs and brainstorming different types of solutions that the client and their audience could benefit from the most. This idea also counts for my love of content creation. Whether it's a campaign video or a new SoMe strategy, I am always excited to research how achieve the best results and use various tools to do my best.

If this sounds good to you, I would be so happy to hear from you. You can reach me via various channels - see more in the Find Me section.

Thank you! :)
